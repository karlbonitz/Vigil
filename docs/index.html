VANTAGE PARSE

The utility parse — interrupt efficiency from your Vantage sessions.

Everything decodes in your browser. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

  1. In game, type /vantage export — a window opens with your session data pre-selected.
  2. Press Ctrl+C to copy it.
  3. Paste it below and hit Build report.

Anonymous. Your character and realm are never sent — only the spell IDs you've confirmed kickable, plus the interrupt that stopped each one so it can be verified, not just trusted.
